Summary:

This message emphasizes that true satisfaction and wisdom come from honoring God, not wealth. Proverbs 17:16 warns that money alone cannot buy understanding. To apply this, one should pray, study the Bible, seek God’s guidance in finances, and live by biblical principles. The takeaway is to prioritize wisdom and faith over material riches.

Wisdom Worth More Than Wealth

"Why spend money on what cannot truly satisfy? True wisdom begins with honoring God. Without Him, no amount of wealth can buy understanding. Let’s build our lives on a foundation of faith, not just finances.

‘Why should fools have money in hand to buy wisdom, when they are not able to understand it?’ – Proverbs 17:16

How can you apply this in your life?

Spend time daily in prayer and Bible study.

Seek God’s guidance in your financial decisions.

Align your life with biblical principles.

Let’s choose wisdom over wealth and faith over fleeting riches. What steps are you taking to grow in God’s wisdom today?
